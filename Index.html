Frequently Asked Questions

How does VenueConnect verify properties?
We employ a multi-step verification process that includes confirming ownership documentation, reviewing past event history or operational data, and potentially conducting site visits for major venues. Our goal is to ensure the properties listed are legitimate and accurately represented.
What types of sponsorship opportunities are available?
Our platform covers a wide range, including naming rights, jersey sponsorships, event title sponsorships, digital advertising on team/league platforms, experiential activations at venues, broadcast partnerships, and more across various sports like football, basketball, athletics, esports, and rugby.
Is there a fee for brands to use VenueConnect?
Brands can create a profile and browse basic opportunity listings for free. Accessing detailed data, direct messaging capabilities, and premium features may require a subscription. Please check our pricing page (link placeholder) for current details.
How do properties list their sponsorship packages?
Property owners can create a detailed profile through their account dashboard. This includes uploading venue/event information, audience demographics (if available), defining specific sponsorship packages (e.g., Gold Tier, Official Partner), setting price ranges, and uploading supporting media like photos or videos.
